About Me

I am a 3rd-year BS-MS student at IISER Kolkata, majoring in Physics with a deep passion for Astrophysics, especially Solar Physics. I love delving into research, coding, and uncovering the mysteries of the Sun through both observational and computational techniques.

Outside the world of science, I enjoy immersing myself in music, gaming, outdoor games, capturing moments through photography, and exploring new places through travel.

Research Interests

  • Solar physics: small-scale solar features, magnetic fields, sunspots, coronal mass ejections
  • Plasma astrophysics and magnetohydrodynamics (MHD)
  • Observational & computational astronomy using space and ground-based solar telescopes
  • Data analysis and simulations in astrophysics using Python (SunPy, Astropy)
