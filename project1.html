Solar Magnetic Dynamics: Emergence and Cancellation of Flux

Summer 2024 – Indian Institute of Astrophysics, Bangalore

Supervisor: Dr. Tanmoy Samanta

This project explores the methodologies and findings related to the calculation of photospheric flux emergence and cancellation rates. Using observational data from SO/PHI and advanced data processing techniques, we quantify the rates at which magnetic flux emerges and cancels in the solar photosphere. These insights shed light on the role of magnetic flux in shaping solar activity and dynamics.
